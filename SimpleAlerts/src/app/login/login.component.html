SimpleAlerts
Never miss another shoutout.
Designed to help organize your alerts by meaningful filters.
Want to make sure to be alerted right away when someone drops a mind blowning donation? Set a threshold to bump the event to the top.
Don’t want to forget your high quality cheers? Set a threshold to have SimpleAlerts bump those massive cheers to the top of the list.
Have a couple minutes passed since you shouted out your newest followers? SimpleAlerts can bump these alerts to the top of the list!
Want to make sure you don’t miss a Tier 3 sub or a 24 month resub? SimpleAlerts can bump these alerts to the top of your list so you don’t have to.
Made for the community.
Open Source. Always Improving.
SimpleAlerts is completely open source. Found a bug or have a killer idea? Contribute now!
