SimpleAlerts
Never Miss Another Shoutout.
SimpleAlerts is a streaming tool designed to help organize your alerts by meaningful filters.
Want to make sure to be alerted right away when someone drops a mind blowning donation? Set a threshold to bump the event to the top.
Don’t forget your high quality cheers! Set a threshold to have SimpleAlerts bump those massive cheers to the top of the list.
Has it passed a couple minutes since you have shouted out your newest followers out? Let SimpleAlerts bump these alerts to the top with a timeframe you set!
Want top make sure you don’t miss a Tier 3 sub or a 24 month resub? SimpleAlerts can bump these alerts to the top of your list so you don’t have to.
